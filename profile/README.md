# 🐵 Money Monkey

**Money Monkey** is an open-source platform for managing personal and family finances with a focus on simplicity, transparency, and powerful financial insights. 

Track everything in one place—from daily expenses and shared family budgets to investments, loans, real estate, cryptocurrencies, and long-term financial goals. Whether you're managing your own finances or collaborating with your family, Money Monkey helps you understand your financial health and make better decisions.

### ✨ Features

* 💰 Income & expense tracking
* 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Shared family accounts and budgets
* 💳 Bank accounts, cash, and credit cards
* 📈 Stocks, ETFs, bonds, crypto, and dividends
* 🏠 Real estate and other assets
* 💸 Loans, mortgages, and debts
* 🌍 Multi-currency support with exchange rates
* 📊 Interactive dashboards and financial reports
* 🔒 Secure, cloud-native architecture
* 📱 Modern web and iPhone applications

### 🏗️ Built with Modern Technologies

Money Monkey is designed as a scalable cloud-native platform powered by:

* .NET 10
* React + TypeScript
* PostgreSQL
* RabbitMQ
* gRPC
* Redis
* Kubernetes
* Docker

### 🎨 Design Philosophy

Clean, modern, and intuitive—with subtle comic-inspired visuals and the friendly **Money Monkey** mascot to make financial management more enjoyable without compromising professionalism.

### 🌟 Our Mission

Our goal is to build one of the most comprehensive, privacy-focused, and developer-friendly personal finance platforms, making it easy for individuals and families worldwide to take control of their money.

We welcome contributions from developers, designers, testers, and anyone passionate about building great financial software together.
